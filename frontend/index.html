A community for every type of reader.

Our Mission

Letterboxd reshaped how people connect over movies, a sleek, social platform where you can log, review, and discover through community. We're bringing that same idea to literature, but with a modern twist designed for today's readers: not just novels, but also plays, short stories, and essays. Our app will let users: Log their reading journey quickly, from anywhere. Review and rate works to spark meaningful conversation. Browse and follow public collections to see what friends, colleagues, or even critics are reading, and get inspired by curated lists. Connect socially by following other readers, sharing insights, and building a literary community that feels alive and approachable. Unlike Goodreads, which feels dated and transactional, our platform is clean, intuitive, and designed for busy young professionals who want discovery, connection, and inspiration without the clutter. In short: we're making reading fun and shareable again, a platform that fits seamlessly into everyday life, whether you're looking for your next subway read, curating a list of plays you've loved, or exploring someone else's favorite short stories.
